# World Cup 2026 Family Bracket Challenge — Rules & Scoring

## How It Works

Picking happens in **two phases** so that knockout picks are always based on real teams, not guesses on top of guesses.

### Phase 1 — Group Stage Picks
**Deadline: Before the first match of the tournament.**

For each of the 12 groups, pick:
- Which team finishes **1st**
- Which team finishes **2nd**

Picks are locked once the tournament begins. No changes (unless you have a Kid Power that allows it).

### Phase 2 — Knockout Bracket Picks
**Deadline: Before the Round of 32 begins.**

Once the group stage is complete and the real bracket is set, everyone picks the knockout rounds fresh — Round of 32 all the way through the Final. You're picking real matchups between teams you just watched for three weeks.

---

## Scoring

### Group Stage

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Team you picked correctly advances (either position) | **2 pts** |
| Team is also in the correct position (1st or 2nd) | **+1 pt bonus** |

- Max per team: **3 pts**
- Max per group: **6 pts** (3 per team × 2 picks)
- Max group stage total: **72 pts** (6 pts × 12 groups)

### Knockout Rounds

Points double with each round to keep everyone in contention deep into the tournament.

| Round | Points per correct pick |
|-------|------------------------|
| Round of 32 | **3 pts** |
| Round of 16 | **6 pts** |
| Quarterfinals | **12 pts** |
| Semifinals | **24 pts** |
| Final | **48 pts** |

Max knockout total: **3+6+12+24+48 = 93 pts** (if you sweep every round)

### Champion Bonus

Before the tournament begins, everyone names their **champion pick**. If your champion wins the whole thing, you earn **+50 bonus points** at the end — regardless of how the rest of your bracket looks. This keeps everyone watching the Final.

### Shootout Mercy Rule

If a team you picked loses in a **penalty shootout**, you receive **half points** for that pick. Getting eliminated in the cruelest way possible shouldn't also cost you full points.

## Kid Powers

Kids age 10 and under get special powers to use during the tournament. Each power can only be used **once** unless it says otherwise. You have to tell a grown-up before you use one!

| Power | How It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| **Magic Swap** | Changed your mind? Swap one pick for a different team. Say it out loud before the game starts. One time only! |
| **Extra Life** | Your champion got knocked out? Pick a new one! If they win it all, you still get points. Not as many — but hey, still points! |
| **Double Down** | Before any game, shout "DOUBLE DOWN!" Win it and get TWICE the points. You only get one shout. Make it count! |
| **Free Pass** | After a round ends, pick your worst score and make it disappear. Poof. Gone. See ya! |
| **Copycat** | Copy anyone's pick for one game. If they're right, you're right too — and they keep their points too! |
| **Rainbow Pick** | Pick a team for any silly reason. Favorite color. Coolest flag. A feeling in your tummy. Tell a grown-up first. Get it right? TRIPLE points! |
| **Comeback Chip** | In last place? You get 3 free bonus points at the start of that round. This happens every time you're last. You're never out! |
| **Heart Pick** | Pick your favorite team ever. Every time they win any game — even ones you didn't pick — you get a bonus point. Cheer loud! |
| **Lucky Guess** | Close your eyes. Flip a coin. Point randomly. A grown-up watches to keep it real. If your wild guess is right? TRIPLE points. |
| **Fuse** | Add your score and a friend's score together. Still losing? You can FUSE and become one team! You share points, picks, and powers from now on. No un-fusing. Choose wisely! |

## General Rules

- All picks must be submitted before the relevant deadline or they will not count.
- Picks are final once locked (except where a Kid Power allows otherwise).
- In case of a tie in the overall standings, the tied players can choose to share the title as co-champions — or settle it the fun way: all the kids in the game secretly hold up some fingers behind their backs. The tied players each guess whether the total number of fingers is **even or odd**. Then the kids reveal. Whoever guesses right wins. If both guess right (or both guess wrong), the kids put up new fingers and you go again.
- Awards are determined after the Final and announced together.
- The Wooden Spoon is non-negotiable and must be accepted with grace.
